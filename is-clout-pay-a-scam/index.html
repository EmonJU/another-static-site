The Paranoia of the Internet

 

As the world around us grows more and more complex, there is plenty that we must be careful of. A number of online users report being caught up in scams that leave them confused about the damages they have sustained. At times, these may be in the form of information theft that may mean that someone on the internet conducts transactions and interacts with your name. At other times, the theft may involve financial repercussions when your payment details are exposed and used for third-party transactions.

The reports of such incidents spread like wildfire and create fear among internet users all around the world. It is this very paranoia that restricts ecommerce sites from really taking route because users are not comfortable providing their details over the internet. They also do not easily trust websites that may offer apparently too-good-to-believe deals.

Viral Cash Pay Influencer Networks

One such deal that internet users may regard too-good-to-be-true is the Viral Cash Pay influencer network. The network is a marketing platform that seeks to connect influencers with eminent advertisers that is beneficial for both the influencers and the brands. Viral Cash Pay is working in this field since 2015 and is one of the leading Influencer Networks around the world.

Why is it Too Good to Be True?

The very reason that many people think Viral Cash Pay is a scam because it apparently gives you a chance to earn easy money. You get paid to get people to simply click over links. For every person you get to click over the said link, you get paid $2. Moreover, if someone signs up after clicking on the link, you get paid $10. The amount of money is apparently too high for just getting people to click over some links and therefore it must be a scam. However, it really is not.

Here is how we know that Viral Cash Pay (viralcashpay.com),  is legitimate:

1.     The Google Check:

Checking for the legitimacy of a website is the easiest with the use of Google. Google’s algorithm generally ranks a website that generates high traffic at the top of the search results. These websites are obviously legitimate. Google would not only not rank Viral Cash Pay at the top of search results if it was a scam but would also mark it unsafe when you do try to visit it. However, Google shows it at the top of search results if you search it over Google and does not warn users trying to visit it. This is one of the checks for a legitimate website.

2.     HTTPS and Website URL:

Https is an extended type of the Http connection type. It stands for Hypertext Transfer Protocol Secure rather than just the Hypertext Transfer Protocol. Sites with an “https” tag in their web address are therefore regarded as more secure and trustworthy rather than an http. Viral Cash Pay works with an “https” tag which not only ensures its users safety against hackers but also marks it as a trustworthy site.

Moreover, the URL of a website is usually an indication into the website purpose. What you should look for are excessive symbols such as points, dashes and so on. You should also look for unfamiliar domain name extensions or incorrect spellings in the URL. Viral Cash Pay has no such shortcomings and is a perfectly made website.

3.     The Appearance and Grammar:

A very important factor used to evaluate whether a site is authentic or not, is to look at its appearance. If a site has too many popups or recurring advertisements, the website is usually illegitimate. Moreover, if the website does not look professional, that is, it has an amateurish feel to it and has an unresponsive interface, the website is definitely a scam.

A factor that gives a website an amateurish feel is its English grammar too. If the website has badly written English with wrong spellings and improper punctuation, the website is not considered authentic. Viral Cash Pay (viralcashpay.com), however, is tailor-made for an influencer network, has a responsive interface and has all the right information for you in perfect English.

How does it Work Though?

But if it is legitimate, how is it even offering such lavish returns for so little effort. The idea is that you help the website generate traffic. This traffic is the main form of revenue for the website. So, when you bring in revenue for the website, you get paid a proportion of that revenue. This is largely why when the website started, it only paid up to a $0.10 per click. But since, it has expanded so much in the short span and is getting better returns, the website pays a bigger proportion to people that get this traffic too. This is why you get paid such lavish returns and why Viral Cash Pay (viralcashpay.com), is not a scam.
